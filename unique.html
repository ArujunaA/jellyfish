Unique and Interesting Jellyfish

There are many kinds of jellyfish that swim in earths waters. There are small ones, big ones, harmless ones, dangerous ones, glowing ones, tall ones, but there are also unique jellyfish with unique physical appearances and abilities. These jellyfish are famous for their feats and powers.

There are many unique jellyfishes out in the ocean. Some jellyfish can deliver a dangerous sting. Other jellyfish can be longer than a blue whale. Other jellyfish have the secret to immortality.

The box jellyfish is a group of jellyfish that deliver one of the deadliest stings in the world. Their sting has the potential to kill an adult human in less than 5 minutes! The box jellyfish lives near Southeast Asia and Oceania and has been spotted on the coasts of Australia. The box jellyfish kills dozens of people each year and injures hundreds. Their poison immediately attacks the heart, causing it to contract. They are also known to cause scars and blemishes.

The lion’s mane jellyfish is the longest thing in the entire ocean. The bell can grow up to 6 feet and it’s tentacles can grow up to 100 ft long! That’s longer than:

  • 1 blue whale
  • 3 school buses
  • 2 Megalodons
  • 15 Michael Jordans

The lion’s mane jellyfish can live near waters up to 0°c and has even been found lurking in the Arctic Ocean next to polar bears! The lion’s mane jellyfish also has the potential to deliver a dangerous sting. It’s sting is not as bad as the box jellyfish, but it can severely harm someone.
