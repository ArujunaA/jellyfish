The Jellyfish Website

Welcome to the Jellyfish Website! Home to the world's most elusive and mysterious creature! In this website you will find information about jellyfish, including their history, their anatomy and their impact on human lives. You will also find fun facts that you may not know about jellyfish! Feel free to look around and discover the wonderful world of Jellyfish!
